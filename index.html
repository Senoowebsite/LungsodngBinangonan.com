Binangonan, Rizal, is a picturesque municipality nestled along the eastern shores of Luzon Island in the Philippines.
Nature enthusiasts are drawn to Binangonan's wealth of outdoor attractions, from tranquil parks and gardens to rugged hills and lush forests.
Binangonan is renowned for its captivating blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and burgeoning urban development.
